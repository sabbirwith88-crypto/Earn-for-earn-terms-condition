6.4 Conversion Rate

1000 Coin = $1 USD. The amount you receive in your Binance/Payoneer account will be the USD equivalent, subject to any fees charged by the payment provider (Binance/Payoneer).

You can view all your withdrawal requests and their status (pending, approved, rejected) in the Withdraw section of the App.

No Refunds/Cancellations: Once a withdrawal request is submitted, it cannot be cancelled. Approved withdrawals are final. If rejected, the Coin is returned to your balance, and you can request again in the next payment window.
